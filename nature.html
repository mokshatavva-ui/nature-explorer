what is nature composed of ?

land

Land is far more than just real estate or dirt; it is the fundamental foundation of the Earth’s biosphere and the stage upon which all terrestrial life unfolds.

water

Water is the fundamental life force of the planet. Covering roughly \(\frac{3}{4}\) of the Earth’s surface, it sculpts our landscapes, regulates global climates, and sustains all living organisms.

fire

Fire is an essential, natural process in many ecosystems that drives biodiversity, recycles vital nutrients, and triggers plant regeneration

wind

Wind is the large-scale movement of air across the Earth's surface, acting as nature's great equalizer. It is primarily driven by the uneven heating of our planet by the sun, which creates temperature and pressure differences.
